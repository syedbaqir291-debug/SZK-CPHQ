Head of Patient Safety Quality & Accreditation | BDS | MBA | CPHQ | LSSBB | FISqua | TQM | LSS Trainer | Healthcare Quality Expert & Trainer | Train the Trainer

She is passionate about driving healthcare excellence through accreditation, quality, and patient safety initiatives. Though a qualified dentist with an acute clinical background, her true passion lies in transforming healthcare systems through quality improvement, accreditation, and patient safety excellence. At Shifa Al Jazeera Medical Group, she contributes to strengthening international accreditation readiness, advancing patient safety practices, and building a culture of quality through cross-functional collaboration.

She is currently leading ACI accreditation efforts, policy development, compliance monitoring, and fostering interdisciplinary staff engagement. With an MBA in Healthcare Administration, and certifications in Lean Six Sigma Black Belt and Total Quality Management, she blends operational expertise with data-driven strategies to drive Continuous Quality Improvement (CQI) across systems. This journey has been deeply enriching — allowing her to integrate clinical insight, process optimization, and organizational leadership to enhance safety, efficiency, and measurable patient outcomes.

Now, she brings this experience together to empower healthcare organizations by simplifying complex compliance processes, driving measurable improvements in quality and safety, and creating meaningful, sustainable operational excellence.
